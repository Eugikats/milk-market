About Milk Connect

Our Mission

Milk Connect was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to eliminate inefficiencies in Uganda's dairy supply chain by directly connecting farmers with milk processing plants. We're dedicated to creating fair market opportunities and ensuring sustainable livelihoods for dairy farmers across Uganda.

The Problem We're Solving

Dairy farmers in rural Uganda often face significant challenges:

  • Unpredictable price fluctuations causing financial instability
  • Limited access to consistent buyers, resulting in milk wastage
  • Exploitation by middlemen who purchase milk at unfairly low prices
  • Logistical difficulties in transporting milk to distant processing facilities
  • Lack of market information and pricing transparency

Our Solution

Direct Connections

Connect farmers directly with processing plants, eliminating middlemen

Price Transparency

Real-time market prices and fair trading practices

Secure Payments

Timely and secure payment processing for all transactions

Efficient Logistics

Optimized transportation and delivery management
